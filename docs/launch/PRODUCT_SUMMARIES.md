# Product Summaries

Last updated: 2026-02-27

## 50-word Summary
`emoj.ie` is a fast, static-first emoji search and copy tool that keeps your workflow local-first. Find emoji by meaning, slang, shortcode, or codepoint, then copy as glyph, Unicode, HTML, or shortcode. Browse clean category, tag, and comparison routes while benefiting from typo-tolerant search and SEO-friendly page architecture.

## 150-word Summary
`emoj.ie` helps people find and copy the right emoji in seconds. The product is built as a static-first experience optimized for speed, clarity, and repeat usage. Search supports meaning, slang, codepoints, and shortcodes, with typo tolerance for common mistakes and short high-intent prefixes. Once you find a result, you can copy in multiple formats including emoji glyph, Unicode, HTML entity, and shortcode.

The site also supports structured exploration through category, subcategory, tag, search-topic, and alternatives routes. This route architecture improves discoverability for both users and search engines while keeping canonical behavior clean. Emoji detail pages provide richer context, related options, and non-commercial usage ideas. Local-first recents, favorites, and theme preference keep the product practical without account friction.

The result is a focused utility for writers, creators, and social teams who need fast emoji lookup with better confidence and lower repeat-search effort.
